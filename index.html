ABOUT US

Nishmi Creations is a MSME-registered trading enterprise based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, dedicated to bringing premium quality home furnishing, wooden furniture, home decor, and wooden artifacts to wholesale buyers and dealers across India.

Founded by Laxmi Rathore, Nishmi Creations blends the rich artistic tradition of Rajasthan with contemporary design sensibilities.

We source and supply a carefully curated range of products that add elegance, warmth, and character to every living space. Our commitment is to quality, reliability, and building long-lasting business partnerships.
